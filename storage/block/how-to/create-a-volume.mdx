<Macro id="iam-requirements" />

<Message type="requirement">
  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com/)
</Message>

<Message type="important">
  This procedure refers to NVMe Block Volumes. To create legacy block volumes, refer the [this page]().
</Message>

1. Click **Block Storage** in the **Storage** section of the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com) side menu. The Block Storage page displays.
2. Click **+Create a volume**.
3. Follow the steps in the creation wizard:
    - Choose an [Availability Zone](/concepts#availability-zone).
    - Configure the volume:
        - Define a name for your volume. A name is provided by default, but can be changed.
        - Select an IOPS ratio.
        <Message type="important">
          You cannot edit the IOPS ratio after you create your volume.
        </Message>

        - Define a volume size.
        <Message type="important">
          The volume size must be of at least 5GB.
        </Message>
    - Select an Instance from the drop-down to attach your volume. This step is optional.
    - Check the estimated cost.
4. Click **Create a new volume**. The volumes overview page displays.
    <Message type="important">
      The volume must be in the same region as the Instance you want to connect it to.
    </Message>
